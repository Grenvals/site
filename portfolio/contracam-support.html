Technical support for ContraCam

Technical support to the website of the ContraCam mobile application.

Review

  • Technology

  • Details

    Support and expansion of the project. Added a technical support to the website of the ContraCam mobile application. The task was to create a technical support section with instructions, manuals, and answers to questions, focus on adaptability, and optimize download speed as much as possible since most of the traffic comes from mobile devices. It was necessary to do it in minimal times since a was necessary to quickly provide users with a resource with manuals for solving the problem. All pages are optimized and verified by WC3 css3, html5 validators, Google Page Speed (green zone).
